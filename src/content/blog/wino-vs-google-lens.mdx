## Why Wino Wins for Shoppers

If you are a botanist, keep using Google Lens. But if you are trying to decorate your room or find a gift?

1.  **The "Coupon Injection":** Wino doesn't just find the link; it copies a discount code to your clipboard before you open the store. Google doesn't do that.
2.  **The "Wishlist":** Google Lens has no memory. Wino saves every scan to your history so you can build a shopping list.
3.  **The "Deep Link":** We link directly to the app (Temu/AliExpress) so the tracking works and the prices are accurate.

## Final Verdict

**Google Lens** is for *identifying* things (Plants, monuments, translations).
**Wino** is for *buying* things (Gadgets, decor, fashion).

Stop paying the "Lazy Tax" by clicking the first Amazon link Google shows you. Scan it properly. Save the money.
