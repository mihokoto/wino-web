## How To Do It (Without Looking Like a Colonial Ghost)

If you haven't seen the tutorial on your FYP yet, here is the cheat sheet:

### 1. Damp, Not Wet
Your hair needs to be 80% dry. If it's soaking wet, you will wake up with damp, sad hair. Use a spray bottle if you need to.

### 2. The Clip Trick
Secure the ribbon at the top of your head with a claw clip. If you don't, it will slide around like a slippery eel while you try to wrap.

### 3. Twist and Wrap
Take a section of hair, wrap it *away* from your face around the ribbon. Add more hair as you go down (like a french braid).

### 4. Sleep on It
Go to bed. Yes, you look a bit like George Washington. Trust the process.
